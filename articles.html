50 Best Art History Blogs

By Art Magazine — Nov 09, 2025 — 8 min read

Curated list of top art blogs exploring culture, ancient art, museums and more

Whether you're a professional art historian, museum curator, masters degree student or just an art enthusiast, there's a lot of great content on art and its history. Below you'll find a carefully curated list of blogs and websites that provide news, analysis, and inspiration.

News and General

These blogs will help you keep up with historical news and learn about the field as a whole. They range from museum reports to commentary on exhibitions.

  • Art History Network — A daily digest of news and commentary across museums and exhibitions.
  • CultureCurator — Interviews with curators and features on contemporary shows.
  • Museum Notes — Deep dives into collections and conservation efforts.

Art Enthusiasts and Experts

Blogs written by art historians, curators and collectors that bring expert insight and research to the public.

Here you can include long-form sections, quotes and block paragraphs just like in the screenshot. For example:

"A museum is a place where time and culture meet." — Curator's Note

Preservation and Protection

Learn about preservation, conservation techniques and the people who keep art safe for future generations.

Ancient Art and Artifacts

From stone age carvings to classical sculptures, these resources explore the origins of artistic expression.

Museums

Major museum blogs and institutional writing — great for students, researchers and visitors planning a trip.

Curator and Museum Studies

Advice and academic posts for those studying museum practice and curatorial work.

Images

Many sites host excellent image archives; always check usage rights before reproducing images in your projects.
